## Overview

This working group serves as the driving force behind the strategic direction and technical aspects of the CDM. We are responsible for establishing technical and modeling guidelines, setting and revising the projectâ€™s strategic roadmap, and vetting proposed changes. Additionally, we approve or establish additional working groups to address specific needs within the CDM ecosystem.

### Responsibilities:

- Define and monitor the overall roadmap, setting annual priorities.
- Decide on support for resourcing initiatives proposed by other working groups (TAWG, CRWG, or DWGs).
- Approve new FINOS Working Groups (WGs).
- Determine matters of Governance & Operating Model for FINOS CDM WGs.
- Provide final release approval.
